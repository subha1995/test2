Why Start Early?

Money Skills Taught Young, Last a Lifetime 🧠

The earlier children learn about money, the better financial decisions they make as adults. Here's why it matters now.

📚

Schools Don't Teach It

Less than 10% of Indian schools include financial literacy in their curriculum, leaving a critical gap in life skills education.

🎂

Habits Form by Age 7

Research shows money habits and attitudes are largely formed by age 7 — making early education essential and impactful.

📈

Compounding Starts Early

A child who starts investing ₹500/month at 15 will have 3x more wealth at 40 than one who starts at 25. Time is money!

🚀

Entrepreneurship Skills

Kids who understand money are 4x more likely to start their own business and create wealth independently in adulthood.

Learning Outcomes

What Your Child Will Learn 🚀

Real-world money skills through games, simulations and hands-on projects.

🏦

Money Basics

How money works, what it represents, and how banks, interest and inflation operate in the real world.

🐷

Saving & Budgeting

Create a personal budget, track spending habits, and build a savings plan that actually works.

📈

Investing Basics

Stocks, mutual funds, SIPs and compound interest explained through fun simulations and real examples.

🏪

Entrepreneurship

Develop a business idea, make a simple plan, calculate profit & loss, and pitch it with confidence.

💳

Smart Spending

Needs vs wants, comparing prices, avoiding impulse buying, and making confident consumer choices.

🌍

Giving & Community

The concept of charitable giving, social impact, and how money can be used to help others.

Curriculum

Complete Course Curriculum 📚

4 fun, age-appropriate modules building money smarts from the ground up.

1

🪙 Money Basics & How Banks Work

What money is, where it comes from, and how banking works — for ages 6+

Weeks 1–4
▼
What is money & barter history
Types of money (notes, digital)
How banks earn & pay interest
Savings accounts & FDs
Income, expenses & profit
Needs vs wants game
India's currency history
UPI, cards & digital money
🛠️ Module Activity

"My First Budget" — Kids create a real personal budget for their monthly pocket money with savings goals

2

🐷 Saving, Budgeting & Smart Spending

Build real money habits through games and simulations — for ages 8+

Weeks 5–9
▼
The 50-30-20 rule for kids
Setting SMART savings goals
Tracking spending habits
Comparing prices & value
Impulse buying traps
Wants vs needs decisions
Emergency fund concept
The power of delayed rewards
🛠️ Module Activity

"30-Day Savings Challenge" — A real 4-week pocket money savings plan with a digital tracker the child maintains

3

📈 Investing, Stocks & Compound Interest

How money grows over time through smart investing — for ages 10+

Weeks 10–15
▼
What are stocks & shares
How the stock market works
Mutual funds & SIPs simplified
Compound interest magic
Risk vs reward explained
Diversification & not putting all eggs in one basket
The Rule of 72
Real stocks simulation game
🛠️ Module Activity

"Virtual Stock Market Game" — Kids pick 5 companies, track their investments for 4 weeks and learn from real market movement

4

🏪 Entrepreneurship & Business Thinking

Start a mini business, make a profit and learn to pitch — for ages 11+

Weeks 16–20
▼
What makes a good business idea
Finding problems to solve
Revenue, cost & profit math
Simple business model canvas
Marketing & pricing basics
Customer feedback & iteration
Social entrepreneurship
Pitching your business idea
🛠️ Module Activity

"Mini Business Launch" — Kids plan, run and pitch a real small business (craft sale, service, etc.) and calculate actual profit
