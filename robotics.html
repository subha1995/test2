Curriculum

Complete Course Curriculum 📚

4 progressive modules from basic electronics to advanced AI robotics.

1

⚙️ Intro to Electronics & Circuits

Understand how electricity works and build your first circuits — ages 8+

Weeks 1–5
▼
What is electricity & voltage
Resistors, LEDs, breadboards
Series and parallel circuits
Introduction to Arduino
Digital vs analog signals
Buttons, switches, buzzers
First Arduino sketches
Light & sound circuits
🛠️ Module Project

"Traffic Light Controller" — A working LED traffic light controlled by Arduino with timing and button inputs

2

🤖 Build Your First Robot

Assemble and program a moving robot from scratch — ages 9+

Weeks 6–12
▼
Motors, wheels and chassis
H-bridge motor drivers
Ultrasonic distance sensors
IR sensors for line following
Robot movement control
Obstacle avoidance logic
Bluetooth remote control
Sensor fusion basics
🛠️ Module Project

"Line-Following Robot" — A self-navigating robot that follows a track, avoids obstacles, and can be remote-controlled

3

🌐 IoT & Smart Systems

Connect robots to the internet and build smart devices — ages 11+

Weeks 13–18
▼
Introduction to IoT
ESP8266/ESP32 WiFi modules
MQTT protocol basics
Temperature & humidity sensors
Cloud data dashboards
Smart home automation
Voice command integration
Mobile app control
🛠️ Module Project

"Smart Plant Monitor" — An IoT device that monitors soil, water, light and sends alerts to your phone when plants need care

4

🧠 AI & Computer Vision

Train AI models and use computer vision in robots — ages 13+

Weeks 19–24
▼
What is Machine Learning
Training your first AI model
Image recognition basics
OpenCV & camera modules
Face & object detection
Gesture-controlled robots
Neural networks simplified
AI ethics for kids
🛠️ Module Project

"AI-Powered Sorting Robot" — A robot that uses computer vision to identify and sort coloured objects automatically
