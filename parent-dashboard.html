❓ Frequently Asked Questions
How do I reschedule a missed class? ▼

If Aarav misses a class, the recording is automatically added to their dashboard within 2 hours. To reschedule a live replacement class, contact your mentor directly via the Mentors page or WhatsApp us. We allow up to 2 reschedules per month free of charge.

How do I upgrade or change the program plan? ▼

You can upgrade to the Explorer or Innovator plan anytime from the Pricing page. Downgrades apply from the next billing cycle. To change specific programs (e.g. swap Robotics for Finance), WhatsApp us and we'll adjust with no extra fee.

When will Aarav receive their certificate? ▼

Certificates are issued automatically when a complete module is finished (all lessons + the module project). You'll get an email notification and can download the PDF from the Progress Report page instantly. Aarav is 2 lessons away from their next certificate!

How do I pause or cancel the subscription? ▼

You can pause your subscription for up to 1 month (e.g. for exams) without losing progress or your spot in the batch. To cancel, email us or WhatsApp us at least 5 days before your next billing date. We offer a full refund within 7 days of joining if you're not happy.

Is the platform safe for children? ▼

Absolutely. All classes are live and recorded — you can watch any class your child attended. All mentors are background-verified. There are no ads, no social features, and all data is stored securely. The platform is fully COPPA-compliant and never shares data with third parties.
